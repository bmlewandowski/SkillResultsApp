HELLO AND WELCOME!
This is the QuickStart Guide
Congratulations on getting the SkillResults system! This guide is written to get you up and running quickly and if you need to refer back to it at anytime, just click the "QuickStart" link in the Navigation Bar.

Step One - Setup Your Users

First, you will want to enter users into the SkillTrack system, so they can log in and start to enter their skills, wishlists and educations. Head over to the user managment area and follow the instructions to enter a user one by one, or upload a list of users from a spreadsheet template.

Step Two - Setup your Skill Catalog

Secondly, you will want to review and modify your skill catalog. The Skill Catalog is the list of skills that your users will can pick from. You can add custom skills and modify the catalog to suite the needs of your org.

Step Three - Send out the Welcome Email!

Finally, you will want to go to the User Managment Area and send the "Welcome to SkillResults" email to all your users. This email will provide the login link and instructions for everyone.
